Our Origins

Happy Regular started with a simple goal: to create a drink that feels like comfort in a cup. What began as late-night experiments with tea leaves, milk, and brown sugar quickly turned into a passion for crafting milk tea that brings people together. Every cup we make is rooted in that same feeling—warmth, care, and the belief that even the smallest moments of happiness should be made regular.
